8-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Relax

Arrive in Goa, transfer to your beachside hotel. Enjoy leisure time at the famous Baga Beach followed by a welcome dinner.

Day 2: North Goa Tour

Visit Fort Aguada, Calangute and Baga Beach. Experience thrilling water sports and vibrant beach shacks.

Day 3: Old Goa Heritage

Explore the UNESCO heritage Basilica of Bom Jesus, Se Cathedral, and stroll through the Latin Quarter in Panaji.

Day 4: Dudhsagar Waterfalls

Take a jeep safari to Dudhsagar Falls and visit a spice plantation to learn about local spices and cuisine.

Day 5: Leisure & Wellness

Relax at the beach, enjoy a yoga or spa session, or take an optional boat trip to Butterfly Beach.

Day 6: Grande Island

Snorkel and dolphin watch around Grande Island. Enjoy a beachside BBQ lunch and sunbathing.

Day 7: Markets & Nightlife

Shop at Anjuna flea market, then enjoy a sunset cruise on the Mandovi River with live music and dance.

Day 8: Departure

After breakfast, check out and transfer to the airport with beautiful memories of Goa.

Accommodation
